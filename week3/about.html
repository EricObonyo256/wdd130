About Our Company

Whitewater Adventures is a locally owned rafting company led by Eric Obonyo. Located in Nakawa, Kampala, Uganda, our goal is to offer thrilling, safe, and unforgettable experiences on Uganda’s rivers.

Meet the Team

  • Eric Obonyo – Founder & Lead Guide

  • Marion Akello – River Guide
  • Grace Apio – Guest Services

Our Mission

To make river rafting accessible, safe, and wildly fun for locals and tourists alike. Whether you're a first-time rafter or a thrill-seeking veteran, we’ve got an adventure for you.
