About Whitewater Adventures

Founded and led by Eric Obonyo, Whitewater Adventures is based in Nakawa, Kampala, Uganda. We offer thrilling, safe, and unforgettable rafting experiences for individuals and groups of all skill levels.

Our History

Since our founding in 2015, Whitewater Adventures has been sharing the excitement and beauty of Uganda’s rivers with adventurers from all over the world.
