About Oeric Rafting Adventures

Our mission is to provide thrilling and safe rafting experiences while fostering a love for nature. Join us to create unforgettable memories with family and friends!

About Our Company

Oeric Rafting Adventures is a locally owned rafting company led by Eric Obonyo. Located in Nakawa, Kampala, Uganda, we combine passion, safety, and sustainability to bring you the best river adventures.

Our History

Founded in 2015, Oeric Rafting Adventures began with a dream of sharing the beauty and thrill of Uganda’s rivers. Starting with just one raft and a lot of passion, we have grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies in East Africa.
