About Our Company

Oeric Rafting Adventures is a locally owned rafting company led by Eric Obonyo. Located in Nakawa, Kampala, Uganda, our goal is to offer thrilling, safe, and memorable experiences on Uganda’s rivers.

Our History

Founded in 2015, Oeric Rafting Adventures began with a dream of sharing the beauty and thrill of Uganda’s rivers. Starting with one raft and a lot of heart, we've become one of the most trusted rafting companies in the region.
