Oeric Rafting Adventures - Site Plan

1. Purpose Statement

The purpose of the Oeric Rafting Adventures website is to showcase adventure trips, provide clear trip information, and encourage visitors to book trips online. The site highlights scenic locations, trip types, pricing, and safety guidance, ensuring potential customers can make informed decisions and easily contact the business.

2. Target Audience

  • Primary: Adventure seekers aged 18–45, interested in rafting, hiking, and camping.
  • Secondary: Families and groups planning weekend or multi-day getaways.

Audience needs: clear trip info, images, pricing, navigation, and booking options.

3. Personas & Scenarios

  • Jane, 28: Adventure traveler, wants weekend rafting trip; browses on mobile, expects fast info.
  • Mark, 35: Teacher planning family camping; uses desktop to compare trips and book online.
