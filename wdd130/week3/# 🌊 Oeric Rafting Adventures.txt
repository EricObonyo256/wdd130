# 🛶 Oeric Rafting Adventures

Welcome to the official website for Oeric Rafting — your ultimate guide to white-water thrills, riverside escapes, and... one unexpectedly photogenic cat. 🐈🌊

---

## 📁 Project Overview

This site was created to showcase:
- Rafting trip options
- Oeric’s story and mission
- Contact details for adventure seekers
- A surprise page with our feline mascot
